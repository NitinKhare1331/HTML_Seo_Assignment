SEO stands for search engine optimization. It helps to optimize the website so that they can have a better ranking on search engines like google , yahoo , etc. By increasing the ranking of the website it gradually increases traffic on the website and thus increases the revenue through that particular website.
Particularly in HTML document tags and meta tags are used which help search engines to optimize the website.

Importance of SEO:-
  Increases brand visibility

  Drive more traffic to the website

  Establish a brand as an authority in an industry

  Build brand credibility and trust

  Generate quality leads

  Focus the entire marketing funnel
  
  Answer potential customers' questions
